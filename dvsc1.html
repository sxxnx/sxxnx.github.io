Data Visualization Society Challenge

Summary

Here is my contribution to the first Data Visualization Society Challenge. The data includes the first 3500 members of the Slack. Each member had to evaluate him/herself in 3 main topics : Data, Visualization and Society (each one divided into 3 sub-topics). The dataset is composed of the mean score of each member in the 3 main topics and the position (lat, long) and time each member subscribed.

Reading the chart

I divided the data between all continents, the algorithm to sort by continent may not be perfect, that is why there is some data falling into the "Other" category (or maybe people for outer space ;)).

The main goal of the chart is to visualize where the members come from and how they evaluate themselves in both categories : Data, Visualization and Society.

If you hover the different continents you get some extra info about each one, such as the mean score for each category and the number of members.
